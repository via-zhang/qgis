Information about the data:

File Name Description Source
fire_points Points of fires from 1900s to recent CAL FIRE
roads Lines of major roads in California US Census Bureau TIGER/Line
fire_perimeters Outlines of burned area for 2025 fires Wildland Fire Interagency Geospatial Services (WFIGS) Group
satellite_[date] Satellite images for three different days of the Palisades Fire:- November 28, 2024- January 12, 2025- March 8, 2025 Sentinel-2 from the Copernicus Browser

Vector Data

Info

“Vector data” refers to locations, places, or other parts of the Earth’s surface that are stored as points, lines, and polygons. We will visualize fire points, road lines, and burned areas from fires in Los Angeles.

Raster Data

Info

“Raster data” refers to areas of the Earth’s surface that are stored as values on a grid (pixels).

Satellite Images

We will visualize satellite images from Sentinel-2 from before and after the Palisades Fire, a devastating fire that started burning Los Angeles on January 7, 2025.
